About My Work

The journey began in early lockdown, 2020 - 2021. I ventured into rendering basic images based off of an io game (krunker.io) that was big at the time. I continued to make images, inspried myself and joined behance. This was the place that I was to upload my artwork. I wasn't great, but I wasn't too bad. Looking back now though I can confirm that I was god awful.
By the time it was 2022, I had gained a bit of experience and decided to try a different game. I looked around for different games that I thought looked good. Nothing.
Throughout this time I attempted to strengthen my raw photoshop skills. I took upon pasting png's of random things and pasting things into a photoshop document and blending and finding out new techniques.
Around 2023 - 2024, I was doing Kirka and Kour artwork. Kour was a newly released game by a company called Legion Platforms. The developers migrated from Krunker near it's death. I found the assets and started making images using them. I'm kind of skipping a period where I developed my artwork a lot. Before this I moved from randomly photoshopping images to expanding my knowledge in image making. I analysed other IO game artists such as Hishe, 14Julink (official Krunker 3D artist at the time), and others, seeing their techniques. This helped a lot. I was in love with a type of style that included, soft vibrant colours, with a lot of soft shadows and cartoonish style.
It took me about 6 months in 2024 to get to this desired skill right. Anyways, after exploring the Kour movement, I went to doing Kirka.io, which was quite successful. This was almost the point of my peak. After this I decided to compete with my younger self and recreate the photoshopped images. To be honest, my younger self won. Anyways. I'm currently working on a project for a new game that is in development. Apart from that I don't know what else to say about my journey.
